## Reporting a Vulnerability

Please report suspected vulnerabilities by emailing **hello@kongmy.dev**.

To help us triage quickly, please include:
- A clear description of the issue and potential impact
- Steps to reproduce (or a proof of concept, if available)
- Affected version(s), commit hash, or environment details
- Any known mitigations or workarounds

### What to expect
- **Acknowledgment:** within 3 business days
- **Initial triage/update:** within 7 business days
- We will keep you informed on remediation progress and release planning

### Disclosure process
Please do **not** disclose the vulnerability publicly until we have investigated
and released a fix (or agreed on a disclosure timeline with you). We follow a
coordinated disclosure process and will credit reporters who wish to be acknowledged.
